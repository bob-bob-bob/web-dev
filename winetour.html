New Zealand’s classic vineyard tour, this tour traces its way around the bays, hills and ridges of the Marlborough Sounds.

Overview

This vineyard tour offers cyclists (and walkers) a chance to delve deep into the wines of Marlborough Sounds, one of the country’s most beautiful waterways. Starting at historic Ship Cove in the outer sounds, the tour skirts around bay after bay, linked by saddles and ridges offering stupendous views and excellent wine
